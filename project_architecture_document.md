## 1. Ethical Framework
- Definition of ethics in AI
- Importance of ethical considerations in cognitive enhancement
- Principles guiding ethical decisions:
  - Beneficence: The aim to enhance cognitive functionalities for good.
  - Non-maleficence: Avoiding harm to individuals and society.
  - Justice: Ensuring fair access to cognitive enhancement technologies.
  - Accountability: Establishing responsibility for outcomes and impacts.
  
## 2. Cognitive Enhancement Goals
- Definition of cognitive enhancement
- Specific enhancement objectives:
  - Improving memory and learning efficiency
  - Boosting problem-solving skills
  - Enhancing emotional intelligence and decision-making
- Long-term vision for cognitive enhancement in society

## 3. Human-Centered Design Principles
- Definition of human-centered design
- Principles to guide the design process:
  - User involvement: Engaging end-users in design and feedback phases.
  - Accessibility: Ensuring products are usable by people with varying abilities.
  - Usability: Designing intuitive and easy-to-use systems.
  - Iterative development: Continuous improvement based on user testing and feedback.
